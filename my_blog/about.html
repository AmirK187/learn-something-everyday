What Mech to Dev is?

Mechanic to Developer is a personal blog about transitioning from being a Qaulified Artisan to a Web developer.
The process of the transition will be documented and added as I strive to improve on a skill that is out of my element of work.
Having worked in construction, there has become a mundane environment and lack of creativity.
While still young and have the hunger to learn and keep the hamster wheel turning, becoming a developer is the ideal dream job.
